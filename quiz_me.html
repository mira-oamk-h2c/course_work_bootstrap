Questions

How well do you know Berlin and it's history?

What is the population of Berlin?

Correct answer: ~ 3,8 million. Berlin is the largest city of Germany by both area and population. Its 3,769,495 inhabitants (December 2019) makes it the most-populous city of the European Union.

When was the Berlin Wall demolished?

Correct answer: 1989. The evening of 9th of November 1989 is known as "The night the Wall came down". The official demolition of the Wall began in June 1990, followed by German reunification, which formally took place on 3rd of October 1990.

What was the name of the best-known Berlin Wall crossing point?

Correct answer: Checkpoint Charlie. Checkpoint "C" was the single designated crossing point (on foot or by car) between East Berlin and West Berlin for foreigners and members of the Allied forces.

In how many districts is Berlin divided to?

Correct answer: 12. Berlin's twelve districts or boroughs (German: Bezirke, pronounced [bəˈtsɪʁkə]), each have their own local government, though all boroughs are subject to Berlin's city and state government.

During the cold war, Berlin was the capital of:

Correct answer: East Germany. The capital of West Germany was Bonn. West Germany was officially called the Federal Republic of Germany, retrospectively designated as the "Bonn Republic".
